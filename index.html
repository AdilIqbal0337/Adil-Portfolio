About Me

Hi, my name is Adil Iqbal, and I’m a passionate and self-driven Web Developer with a creative mindset and a love for building modern, responsive websites. My academic journey began at Jackson Secondary School in Keamari, where I completed my secondary education. To further strengthen my technical foundation, I enrolled in a local coaching center and successfully earned my Diploma in Information Technology (DIT). While pursuing my passion for technology, I also started my Islamic education at Jamia Tur Rasheed, which has deeply enriched my character and perspective. Alongside this, I got admitted to Al-Biruni Intermediate College, where I am currently studying in 2nd year of Computer Science. To gain practical experience, I completed a three-month web development internship at Digitech Infra Associates. During this time, I worked on several projects and gained hands-on experience in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5, and JavaScript. This internship played a vital role in polishing my skills and building my confidence as a developer. I am constantly learning and growing, eager to take on new challenges and contribute to impactful projects. My goal is to become a well-rounded developer who not only builds websites but creates meaningful digital experiences.
